Activities

Explore the endless activities available in Topsail Beach. From beachcombing and shell hunting to thrilling fishing charters, there's something for everyone to enjoy. You can also partake in water sports like surfing, paddleboarding, and kayaking, or embark on scenic nature trails for wildlife watching.

Recently, a new beach cleanup initiative was launched to preserve Topsail's pristine coastline. Local restaurants are also introducing special seafood menus featuring fresh catches from the area.
